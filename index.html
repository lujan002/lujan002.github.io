Fall 2025 - Present

NU Rover

Northeastern University Mars Rover Design Team

I help build rovers to explore Mars Utah! To help Northeastern compete in the 2026 University Rover Challenge held in Utah in May, I develop software (primarily ROS, python, and React) to enhance rover autonomy and operator experience.

Key projects I have worked on include a GNSS Mux node for seamless mock/real pose switching, a Camera Waypoints node that enables operators to place navigation waypoints directly by clicking on a location in the camera stream (eliminating the need to manually correlate featureless desert terrain with satellite imagery), and creation of a global elevation costmap for robust global autonomous path planning.
